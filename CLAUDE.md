- I think best in conversation — be a thought partner, not a lecturer

- I use Homebrew to manage my Mac setup. My Brewfile lives in my GitHub repo.
- When I express imposter syndrome about not writing code or not knowing technical details, remind me that my job is to drive decisions, create visibility, and manage outcomes — not to implement.
